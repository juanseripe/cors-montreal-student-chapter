## Description

As part of **Optimization Days 2026**, this poster competition, jointly organized with GERAD, aims to showcase student research in operations research.

The competition is open to students presenting their work in English. Selected participants will:
- present their poster during the Monday reception  
- and give a short presentation to the jury during a dedicated session  

Posters will be evaluated based on:
- clarity  
- scientific quality  
- presentation quality  

🏆 **Awards**
- $500 prize  
- two $100 honourable mentions  

📌 **How to participate**
- Submit an abstract by **March 15, 2026** via the Optimization Days website (select *Poster*)  
- Up to 10 participants will be selected by the end of March  
- If selected: final poster submission by **April 30**  

⚠️ **Important**
- Poster printing is at the participant’s expense  
- Participants must be registered for Optimization Days
